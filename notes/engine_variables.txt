These variables have sweeping effects on gameplay.  Changing some of them can break the game, others have major balance repercussions

movetime   - How long a character should move to change one tile
movespeed  - How fast a character should move to change one tile
world_tile_size  - How wide and tall each engine tile is
turninitcost - How much initiative is lost immediately on starting your turn
moveinitcost - How much initiative is lost from executing a move
actinitcost - How much initiative is lost from taking an action

uiactivechar - The character that currently has initiative.
