Things that need to be saved for a working save system

DSGrid    global.charstat (All Character Stats)
DSMap     global.events (Tracks where the player is in story, what chests opened, etc.)
DSMap     global.inventory (Track inventory)
DSGrid     global.equipment (Track equipment)
DSMap     global.abilities.characterid (If ability customization is going to be a thing)


Additionally, for an in-combat save

global.initiativecycle (Current Turn)
DSGrid global.battlemap (Current Positions on Map)
DSGrid global.terrainmap (Current Terrain on Map) [If modded]
