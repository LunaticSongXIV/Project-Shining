Controls

Mouse:  Move with Cursor, LMB - Confirm, RMB - Cancel, MMB - Release, Main Menu - None
Keyboard:  Move with WSAD -or- arrows, Space Confirm, BkSp Cancel, TAB Release, ESC Menu
Controller:  Move with Arrow Pad or Stick, A Confirm, B Cancel, X Release, Start Menu

Extra Functions:

Jump Next Ally  - >, RB
Jump Next Enemy - ], RT
Jump Prev Ally  - <, LB
Jump Prev Enemy - [, LT
